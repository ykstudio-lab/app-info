Specifications

Vibration Modes

  • CONTINUOUS: Constant, steady vibration.
  • SLOW: Relaxing slow-paced pulses.
  • FAST: Quick, refreshing pulses.
  • WAVE: Surging vibration with varying intensity.
  • INTERACTIVE: Intensity changes with device movement.

Key Features

  • Intensity Dial: Fine-tune power from 0-100%.
  • Turbo Boost: Instant max power on long-press.
  • Auto Stop: Automatically turns off after 1 min of inactivity.
  • Wake Lock: Prevents screen sleep during use.

Requirements

  • Android: 5.0 or higher
  • iOS: 12.0 or higher
